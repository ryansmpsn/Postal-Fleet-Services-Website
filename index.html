About PFSI

Who We Are

  • We are an organization that offers superior and personalized service to the United States Postal Service, with safe and efficient associates; utilizing well-maintained equipment.

What We Do

  • ​We provide a mutual respect of our associates’ responsibilities in the organization. It is only through each individual associates efforts that we can provide superior service and it is through each individual associate’s mutual support to each other that the company will prosper. We expect all associates to preserve and build upon our respected reputation for courtesy to the United States Postal Service personnel and all of our other business partners, and the general public, prompt delivery of services sought, safe operations and compliance with all governmental regulations involving our industry.

We are a family organized into a corporate enterprise, dedicated to serving the United States Postal Service with superior quality transportation and supply chain services that provide for an adequate return on investments to sustain the future growth of the corporation and security for employees.

Core Values

Customer Service

Postal Fleet Services dedicates itself to providing the United States Postal Service with 99.8% on-time delivery performance. Although we have not achieved this worthy goal, our employees continuously strive to reach this endeavor with a current on-time delivery service. Postal Fleet Services’ provides its drivers with the most advanced technology available in the marketplace to help them safely reach their goal.

Environmental Sustainability

As an environmentally concerned corporate citizen, Postal Fleet Services’ has introduced Alternative Fuel Vehicles into its fleet of vehicles. With multiple alternative fuel vehicles in the marketplace, we will evaluate them as the viability of those technologies become readily available to determine if they fit within our environmental goals. Should they prove an excellent economical and efficient fit, we will use them in our expanding fleet as we continue to grow. We want to increase our environmentally friendly footprint with a cleaner environment for future generations.

Safety

As a provider of transportation to America’s highest service ranked governmental agency, we are ever mindful of the need to protect the safety of our employees and the safety of the general public with which we share the roadways. In order to accomplish this, we spec our tractors and straight trucks with Autonomous Mitigation Systems (lane departure and automatic braking systems); back up cameras and front and rear cab cameras. As more safety technology is introduced to the market, we will adopt that technology as it is proven to enhance safe driving for all concerned.
